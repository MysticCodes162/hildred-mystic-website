The Evolution of AI: From 1962 to the Future

1. The Birth of Artificial Intelligence (1962)

AI began its journey in 1962 when researchers first attempted to create machines capable of thinking like humans. These systems were simple — solving checkers or math problems — but they marked the beginning of a revolution.

2. How Gaming Shaped AI

Early video games like Tennis for Two, Pac-Man, and Donkey Kong forced developers to build smarter algorithms. Pathfinding, decision-making, and learning systems were born here.

3. The Rise of Arcades & Home Consoles

The 80s and 90s pushed AI forward through simple yet challenging games that required fast adaptation and pattern recognition.

4. Early 2000s: Online Gaming + Branding Tech

With the internet came MMOs like World of Warcraft. AI now had to control thousands of NPCs at once. Meanwhile, businesses used AI for ads, customer behavior data, and marketing.

5. The Internet Explosion

Search engines, social media, and e-commerce accelerated AI more than anything before. Massive datasets pushed machine learning into every industry.

6. Tesla & the Mobile Revolution

In 2014, Tesla changed everything with self-driving technology. Mobile phones advanced AI further with Siri, Google Assistant, facial recognition, and real-time translation.

The journey isn’t over — we’re only at the beginning.
