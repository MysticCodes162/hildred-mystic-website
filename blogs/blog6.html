The History and Growth of Artificial Intelligence

AI research began in the 1960s with machines solving simple problems. Early games like Tennis for Two provided a testing ground for algorithms, paving the way for modern AI.

The 1980s–90s arcade and home games challenged AI development, leading to algorithms for pattern recognition, decision-making, and expert systems that impacted real-world industries.

The internet’s rise accelerated AI learning via data, collaboration, and open-source sharing. Search engines and recommendation systems became early AI success stories.

Modern milestones include Tesla’s self-driving cars and mobile AI applications, merging AI with daily life, shaping transportation, communication, and personal technology.
